Portfolio

Your personal collection of all the investments you own

Portfolio in one minute

Imagine a bag where you keep all your investments – some shares, some mutual funds, some gold, some fixed deposits. That bag is your portfolio. It shows the full picture of your financial investments in one place.

A little deeper

Why portfolio matters

Knowing what you own helps you understand your total risk and expected return.

Diversified portfolio

Spreading money across stocks, bonds, gold, real estate = not all eggs in one basket.

Review regularly

Markets change. Your portfolio should be reviewed every 6-12 months to check if it still matches your goals.

Asset allocation

How much in equity vs debt vs gold vs cash depends on your age, goals, and risk appetite.

How people making money from this

Tracking tools

Apps like Zerodha, Groww, and INDmoney let you track your entire portfolio in one dashboard.

Rebalancing

If equity grows too large a share, sell some and buy more debt to maintain balance.

Risks

A poorly built portfolio can have hidden concentration in one sector. Diversify smartly
